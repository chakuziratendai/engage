Keynote and Opening General Session

Reimagine Work in the Era of AI

Join IBM Collaboration Solutions for a peek into the future of work. No matter your line of business, organizational mission, or role within the company—how you, your teams and your external ecosystem get work done can make all the difference in building a competitive advantage. This session will share IBM's vision for the future of work and how our partnerships and technological innovations, including AI-infused collaboration tools, are making the future a reality now. Understand how creating digital work hubs that unite people, content and operations with the speed and efficiency of the cloud can revolutionize everyday interactions, tasks and processes and transform your business.

A new chapter begins: Domino V10

In the digital era, empowering the workforce with the ability to reengineer their workflows, processes and activities into more competitive and effective outcomes for the business is essential. In this session, we will share our vision for developers refreshing and developing new Domino apps that are foundational to automating processes that free up workers to pursue higher-value activities. Plus, see what businesses using mail and chat on their desktops and mobile devices can expect in 2018.
